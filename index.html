What We Do
Bar Consultancy & Event Bar Catering.

Two focused, world-class services. From mobile cocktail bar hire in London to full beverage program consultancy, delivered with precision, creativity, and craft.

Leading UK Bar Consultancy for High-Performance Hospitality Concepts

We design, develop and optimise bar programmes for hotels, restaurants, cocktail bars and drink brands. From concept to execution.

01
01
Strategy & Concept

Concept Development & Bar Strategy

Strategic bar concept development and positioning designed to create distinctive, market-driven hospitality experiences.

  • Bar concept creation tailored to your venue and target market
  • Brand positioning for strong market differentiation
  • Market research and feasibility studies
  • End-to-end project management from concept to execution
  • Business development and growth strategy for long-term scalability
02
02
Menu Engineering

Menu Engineering & Development

Innovative, revenue-driven cocktail and beverage menus designed to enhance guest experience, optimise costs, and maximise profitability.

  • Profitable menu design and layout aligned with your concept
  • Cocktail recipe creation and standardisation
  • Beverage cost analysis and margin optimisation
  • Wine, cocktail, and food pairing development
  • Supplier sourcing and procurement planning
03
03
Setup & Training

Bar Setup, Training & Pre-Opening

Hands-on operational support to get your bar team ready to deliver excellence from day one.

  • Staff recruitment guidance and bartender training programmes
  • SOP development and service standards documentation
  • Pre-opening setup, equipment specification and bar layout
  • Live training, mock service, and team coaching
04
04
Operations & Growth

Operations Optimisation & Brand Consulting

Ongoing advisory services to keep your bar running at peak performance and brand-aligned excellence.

  • Operational audit and performance improvement plans
  • Seasonal menu refreshes and range evolution
  • Brand partnership and activation strategy
  • Retail and wholesale beverage brand development

Premium Event Bar Catering Across London & the UK

Fully-equipped mobile cocktail bars for weddings, corporate events, brand activations, and private parties — managed end-to-end by our team.

01
01
Mobile Bar

Mobile Cocktail Bar Hire

Beautifully presented, fully self-contained mobile cocktail bars for any venue or outdoor space.

  • Custom bar units to suit your event aesthetic
  • Premium spirits, mixers and glassware included
  • Setup, operation and breakdown managed by our team
  • Bespoke cocktail menu designed for your event
02
02
Bespoke Menus

Signature Cocktail Creation

Photography-ready, guest-memorable signature cocktails built around your event theme or brand identity.

  • Bespoke event menus with 4–8 cocktails
  • Named and themed serves for weddings and activations
  • Non-alcoholic and low-ABV alternatives
  • Costed, standardised recipes for consistent delivery
03
03
Staffing

Professional Bar Staffing

Trained, experienced, and brand-aligned bartenders who understand that service is as important as the drink.

  • Lead bartenders and bar backs for any event size
  • Uniform and presentation standards
  • Menu knowledge and guest interaction training
  • Staff ratios matched to your guest count and service style
04
04
Logistics

Full Event Management

End-to-end logistics — from venue visit to final breakdown — so your event runs without a hitch.

  • Pre-event venue visit and brief
  • Delivery, setup and full waste management
  • Liaison with venue, caterers and event coordinators
  • Post-event debrief and photo documentation

Our Philosophy

Where Creativity Meets
Practical Hospitality.

Great drinks experiences are never accidental. They are built through clear thinking, operational discipline, and relentless attention to detail.

Guest Experience First

Every decision starts with the guest. How they feel, what they experience, and what they remember when they leave.

Creative but Practical

Beautiful menus and bold concepts that work in real operation, not just on paper or in a pitch deck.

Strong Operational Systems

Behind every great bar is a great system. We build the infrastructure that makes quality consistent and scalable.

Commercially Sustainable

Drinks programs that look premium and perform commercially. Beauty and profitability are not opposites.

Elegant Presentation

From glassware to garnish, every visual detail of how a drink arrives is part of the experience.

Internationally Informed

Experience across India, the United States, and the UK informs every brief. Global perspective, London delivery.
